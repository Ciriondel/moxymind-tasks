    2. Enter a valid username (e.g., standard_user).

    3. Enter the valid password secret_sauce.

    4. Click the "Login" button.

TC-2: Adding and Removing Items from the Cart

Precondition:
    Browser: Google Chrome (or any supported browser)

Steps:

    1. Add all available products to the shopping cart one by one.

    2. Open the shopping cart.

    3. Verify that all selected products are present in the cart.

    4. Remove all products from the shopping cart.

    5. Verify that the cart is empty.

Expected Result:

    • All products are successfully added to the cart initially.

    • All products are successfully removed, leaving the cart empty at the end of the test.

TC-3: Complete Checkout

Precondition:
    Browser: Google Chrome (or any supported browser)

Steps:
    1. Navigate to https://www.saucedemo.com/.

    2. Enter a valid username (e.g., standard_user).

    3. Enter the valid password secret_sauce.

    4. Click the "Login" button

    5. Add a product to the shopping cart.

    6. Open the shopping cart.

    7. Click the "Checkout" button.

    8. Enter the First Name, Last Name, and Zip/Postal Code.

    9. Click the "Continue" button.

    10. Verify that the item name in the order overview matches the item added.

    11 .Click the "Finish" button.

Expected Result:

    • The order confirmation page is displayed.

    • The entire checkout workflow is completed successfully without errors.
